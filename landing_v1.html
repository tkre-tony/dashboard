Landmark REIT 1Q 2026: Rebrand From LMIRT Lands With +13.6% IDR Rental Growth, 87.5% Occupancy, Gearing Down To 40.22% — But Distribution Suspension Continues For 7th Consecutive Cessation On 2016 Perpetuals

Landmark REIT — formerly Lippo Malls Indonesia Retail Trust until the 27 March 2026 rebrand — reported the strongest operating quarter the portfolio has had in years: rental revenue +13.6% IDR (+4.0% SGD), NPI +15.6% IDR (+5.7% SGD) on portfolio occupancy of 87.5% (vs 78.4% Cushman & Wakefield industry average) and 70% of malls above 85%. The January 2026 rights issue cleaned the balance sheet — gearing down 332bps to 40.22%, cash doubled to S$60.3m, US$22.6m 2026 Notes fully redeemed — but at the cost of a 46% NAV-per-unit dilution (4.91¢ → 2.65¢ on the units doubling from 7,696.8m to 16,702.0m). The catch: distributions stay suspended for both Unitholders and the two perpetual tranches, the seventh consecutive cessation on the 2016 perpetual.
