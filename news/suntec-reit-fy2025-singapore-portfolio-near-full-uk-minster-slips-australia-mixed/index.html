Suntec REIT FY2025: Singapore Portfolio Near Full, UK Minster Slips, Australia Mixed

Suntec REIT FY2025 Annual Report (year ended 31 Dec 2025) shows a resilient Singapore portfolio at near-full occupancy, a standout UK occupancy decline at The Minster Building, and diverging trends across the Australian portfolio.

By PropertyAtlas·4 April 2026·1 min read

Suntec REIT FY2025 Annual Report (year ended 31 Dec 2025) shows a resilient Singapore portfolio at near-full occupancy, a standout UK occupancy decline at The Minster Building, and diverging trends across the Australian portfolio. Total portfolio valuation rose +0.7% to S$11.83B.

SINGAPORE — NEAR FULL OCCUPANCY
  • ▸Suntec City Office: Occ +1.0pp to 99.8%; gross rent reversion +25.9% — strongest leasing momentum in the portfolio.
  • ▸One Raffles Quay: Occ -1.6pp to 97.1%; valuation +S$27.0M (+2.0%) to S$1,387.0M.
  • ▸MBFC Towers 1 & 2: Occ -3.1pp to 95.4%; Marina Bay Link Mall retail occ +4.5pp to 98.9%; combined valuation +S$43.9M (+2.4%) to S$1,877.2M.
  • ▸Suntec City Mall: Retail occ +1.1pp to 99.5%; strong full-year rent reversion of 16.2%.
UNITED KINGDOM — MINSTER DRAG
  • ▸Nova Properties: Achieved 100% committed occupancy (FY2024: 99.6%); valuation +S$17.8M (+2.6%) to S$691.0M.
  • ▸The Minster Building: Occ -5.4pp to 85.4% — standout occupancy decline in FY2025; valuation +S$9.9M (+2.2%) to S$468.5M.
AUSTRALIA — DIVERGING PERFORMANCE
  • ▸177 Pacific Highway: Occ held at 100%; valuation -S$23.1M (-4.6%) to S$477.1M on weaker AUD.
  • ▸21 Harris Street: Occ -2.2pp to 97.8%; valuation -S$16.0M (-7.6%) to S$195.1M.
  • ▸Olderfleet 477 Collins Street: Occ held at 100%; valuation +S$5.0M to S$365.3M.
  • ▸Southgate Complex: Office occ -1.5pp to 88.5%; retail occ -15.7pp to 75.3%; valuation -S$4.4M to S$279.9M.
  • ▸55 Currie Street: Occ +4.6pp to 66.0%; valuation +S$4.8M to S$102.3M.
KEY TAKEAWAY

Suntec REIT's FY2025 narrative is Singapore strength offsetting overseas pressure. SG portfolio at near-capacity with positive rent reversions; UK and Australian books face selective softening. NAV per unit S$1.688 (FY2024: S$1.680).
