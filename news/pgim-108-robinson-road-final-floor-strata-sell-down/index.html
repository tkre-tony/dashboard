PGIM’s Final Floor at 108 Robinson Road: S$16.1M Level 3 Sale Closes a Strata Sell-Down at the 12-Storey Freehold Strata-Titled Office Building

The latest URA Realis release brings 13 commercial caveats totalling S$34.2 million — led by the third-floor office at 108 Robinson Road, the last strata lot in PGIM Real Estate’s floor-by-floor exit of the freehold CBD building. Ten of 11 lots are now caveated at a combined S$189.8 million, with the full exit estimated at approximately S$208 million against a S$142.8 million acquisition.
